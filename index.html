Smart Sprinklers

We developed a smart irrigation system using machine learning techniques to predict and optimize soil moisture levels for efficient crop growth. The system considers various factors such as temperature, humidity, solar radiation, wind speed, and soil properties. It utilizes historical weather data and soil sensor measurements to train regression models that estimate soil moisture depletion rates. These models then forecast future moisture levels and recommend irrigation amounts and timing to maintain desired soil moisture. The neural network model showed the highest prediction accuracy. When tested with soil sensors, the system effectively maintained soil moisture within target ranges for various crops and soil types in real-world conditions. This technology has the potential to enhance water-use efficiency and crop yields, demonstrating the feasibility of machine learning in smart precision irrigation.
